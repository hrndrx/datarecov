Our Business Model

Reclaim Data services acts as your local connection to top-tier U.S. data recovery. Whether you accidentally formatted a hard drive, your SSD failed, or your external disk is unreadable, we handle the logistics. Here's how it works:

  1. Consultation & Pickup: You consult us locally and we arrange for you to send your device to us or directly to Salvage Data Recovery upon your preferance.
  2. International Dispatch: Upon payment,We securely ship your device to SalvageData's U.S. recovery lab.
  3. Evaluation & Quote: You receive an evaluation (free in many cases) and a quote.
  4. Recovery: Recovery begins in a secure lab.
  5. Return: The recovered data shall be returned on a brand new hard drive of a preferred size together with the damaged one.
