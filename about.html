Our Business Model

Data Recov acts as your local connection to top-tier U.S. data recovery. Whether you accidentally formatted a hard drive, your SSD failed, or your external disk is unreadable, we handle the logistics. Here's how it works:

  1. Consultation & Pickup: You consult us locally and we arrange to pick up your damaged media.
  2. International Dispatch: We securely ship your device to SalvageData's U.S. recovery lab.
  3. Evaluation & Quote: You receive an evaluation (free in many cases) and a quote.
  4. Recovery: Upon your approval, recovery begins.
  5. Return: The recovered data and/or drive is securely shipped back to you.
