Privacy Policy

At Reclaim My Data, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information you provide (such as name, contact details, and device information).
  • Information related to your data recovery case, including device type, data loss circumstances, and recovery requirements.
  • Technical information collected automatically, such as IP address and browser type, for security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our data recovery services.
  • To communicate with you regarding your case and our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

3. Data Sharing & Security

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • We may share your information with trusted partners (such as our U.S. recovery lab) only as necessary to perform recovery services.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information.
  • You may withdraw consent for data processing at any time, subject to legal and contractual obligations.

5. Data Retention

  • We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any changes.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@reclaimydata.com.
