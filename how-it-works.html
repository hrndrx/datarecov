How Our Recovery Process Works

  1. Client submits a recovery request
  2. We collect or receive the device
  3. Device is packaged and shipped to our US partner
  4. Partner recovers the data in a secure lab
  5. We receive the data and device, test the data
  6. We deliver the data and device back to you
  7. Customer pays and gives feedback
