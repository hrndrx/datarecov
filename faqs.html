Frequently Asked Questions

How does your recovery process work?

We act as your local connection to world-class U.S. data recovery. You consult us locally, we handle the logistics of shipping your device to our U.S. partner (SalvageData Recovery USA), and they perform the recovery in their certified lab. We then return your data and device to you.

Is evaluation free?

Yes, in most cases evaluation is free. You only pay if data is successfully recovered and you approve the quoted cost.

How long does recovery take?

Recovery times vary based on device condition and approval delays. Most cases are completed within 7-14 business days, plus shipping time to and from the U.S.

Is my data safe during international shipping?

We use secure shipping methods and work with a trusted U.S. partner. However, Data Recov is not responsible for damage or loss caused during international transit. We recommend backing up any accessible data before shipping.

What if recovery is not successful?

If recovery is not successful and you decline further services, your device is returned to you. If shipping and logistics were charged in advance, they are non-refundable. We only charge for successful recoveries.

What types of devices do you recover?

We handle hard drives, SSDs, USB drives, phone internal memory, formatted/deleted files, and RAID/NAS systems. All recoveries are performed by our certified U.S. partner facility.

How do I know I can trust you?

We partner with SalvageData Recovery USA, which has over 20 years of experience, operates in over 50 countries, and is trusted by government agencies, corporations, and individuals. We act as your authorized local facilitator.

What are your terms of service?

By using our services, you authorize us to handle and ship your device to the United States for professional evaluation and recovery. We act as an intermediary and logistics provider for SalvageData Recovery USA. All evaluations are free unless stated otherwise.

How do you protect my privacy?

We do not store your recovered data locally without consent. Any personal data shared is used only for communication and logistics. We comply with Uganda's Data Protection laws and align with U.S. privacy standards.
